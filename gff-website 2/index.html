About Green Future Foundation

Founded: 2010, South Africa

What we do: GFF is a non-profit organisation dedicated to environmental conservation and education. We organise tree-planting drives, clean-up campaigns, and sustainability workshops to inspire communities to take action.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To create a greener, healthier planet through community action and environmental awareness.

Vision: A society where people live in harmony with nature.

Our Community

We aim to reach volunteers, sponsors, schools, and environmental activists who share our vision for a sustainable future.

Our Website Goals

  • Recruit more volunteers and donors
  • Share event information and reports
  • Educate visitors through blog posts and resources
  • Showcase our impact with photos and testimonials
