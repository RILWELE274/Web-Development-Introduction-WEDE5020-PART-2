About Green Future Foundation

Together for a greener tomorrow

Our Story

Green Future Foundation (GFF) was established in 2010 in South Africa with the aim of fighting climate change and protecting our environment. Over the years, we have grown into a community-driven organisation that brings people together through conservation projects, clean-up campaigns, and environmental education programs.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To create sustainable communities through environmental action, education, and awareness.

Vision: A future where people and nature live in harmony.

Our Goals

  • Plant 1 million trees across Africa by 2030
  • Educate school learners about climate change
  • Reduce plastic waste in local communities
  • Empower youth with environmental leadership skills

Who We Serve

We focus on engaging:

  • Volunteers and activists passionate about the environment
  • Schools and universities looking for sustainability programs
  • Businesses and sponsors supporting eco-friendly initiatives
  • Communities affected by pollution and climate change
